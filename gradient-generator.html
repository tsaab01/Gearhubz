About Gradients

CSS gradients are powerful tools for creating visually appealing backgrounds:

  • Linear: Straight-line gradient between colors
  • Radial: Circular gradient radiating from a center point
  • Conic: Gradient rotated around a center point

Gradients can enhance your designs with depth and visual interest without using images.
