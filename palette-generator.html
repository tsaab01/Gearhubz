About Color Palettes
  • Monochromatic: Variations in lightness and saturation of a single color
  • Complementary: Two colors opposite each other on the color wheel
  • Analogous: Three colors adjacent to each other on the color wheel
  • Triadic: Three colors evenly spaced around the color wheel
  • Tetradic: Four colors arranged into two complementary pairs
  • Split Complementary: A base color and two adjacent to its complement

Each palette type creates a different mood and visual impact. Experiment to find what works for your project!
