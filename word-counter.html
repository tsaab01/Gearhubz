Text Analysis

The Flesch-Kincaid readability test evaluates text based on:

  • Sentence length (average words per sentence)
  • Word complexity (average syllables per word)
  • Grade level required to understand the text

Higher scores indicate easier readability. Aim for a score of 60-70 for general audiences.
