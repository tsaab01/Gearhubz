What is an Online Image to Text Converter (OCR)?

An online image to text converter, also known as OCR (Optical Character Recognition), is a free web-based tool that extracts readable text from images containing printed or handwritten content. Whether you need to digitize documents, extract text from photos, or convert scanned PDFs to editable text, our tool makes it quick and easy using advanced AI-powered recognition technology.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this image to text converter really free?

Yes, our image to text converter is completely free to use with no limitations, registrations, or watermarks. Convert as many images as you need at no cost. We believe in providing valuable tools without hidden fees or premium restrictions. All processing happens directly in your browser for maximum privacy.

What file formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, and WebP for upload. The extracted text output will be in plain text format (.txt). For best OCR results, use high-resolution images with clear, well-lit text. Scanned documents and photos of printed materials work best, though we can also process handwritten text with reasonable accuracy.

How accurate is the text extraction?

Our OCR technology achieves 95%+ accuracy for clear, printed text in supported languages. Accuracy may vary based on image quality, font type, text size, and language complexity. For best results, use high-resolution images with good contrast and minimal distortion. Handwritten text recognition is available but may have lower accuracy depending on handwriting style and clarity.

Is my privacy protected?

Absolutely. Your images are processed directly in your browser using client-side JavaScript and never uploaded to our servers. We don't store or access your files in any way. All OCR processing happens locally on your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. Once you close the browser tab, all processed data is automatically deleted from your device's memory.
