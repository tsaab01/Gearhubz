About Unit Conversion

Unit conversion is the process of expressing the same property in different units of measurement. It involves multiplying by a conversion factor.

Key principles:

  • Multiplication by a conversion factor (a ratio equal to 1)
  • Cancellation of units during calculation
  • Precision maintenance throughout the process
  • Appropriate rounding of final results

Common applications include scientific research, engineering projects, international trade, and everyday tasks.
