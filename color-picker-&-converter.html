About Color Formats

Different color formats are used in various contexts:

  • HEX (#RRGGBB): Used in web design and CSS
  • RGB (Red, Green, Blue): Used in digital displays and CSS
  • HSL (Hue, Saturation, Lightness): More intuitive for humans to understand
  • RGBA/HSLA: Include alpha channel for transparency

Understanding these formats helps in choosing the right color for your design needs.
