How to Use the PDF Merger & Splitter

Merge PDFs:

  1. Upload - Click "Choose PDF Files" or drag and drop multiple PDF files
  2. Arrange - Reorder files by dragging them in the list (if needed)
  3. Merge - Click "Merge PDFs" to combine all files into one document
  4. Download - Click "Download Processed PDF" to save your merged file

Split PDF:

  1. Upload - Click "Choose PDF Files" or drag and drop your PDF file
  2. Select Pages - Enter page numbers or ranges (e.g., 1-3,5,7-10)
  3. Split - Click "Split PDF" to extract selected pages
  4. Download - Click "Download Processed PDF" to save your split file
