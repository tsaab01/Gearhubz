About Time Zones

Time zones are regions of the Earth that have the same standard time. They follow the boundaries of countries and their subdivisions.

  • UTC (Coordinated Universal Time): Primary time standard by which the world regulates clocks
  • DST (Daylight Saving Time): Practice of advancing clocks during warmer months
  • GMT (Greenwich Mean Time): Mean solar time at the Royal Observatory in Greenwich
  • Local Time: Time observed in a specific region

Understanding time zones is crucial for global communication and travel planning.
